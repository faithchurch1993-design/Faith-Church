About Us

  • FAITH Inc. is a Church that engages in CHRIST`s mandate, Christian walk and social. With our faith directed to God and we offered as service of love for him. FAITH gives our church community an opportunity to put faith into practice and share Christ`s LOVE and COMPASSION. FAITH.Inc was founded in 1993 formerly known as Faith Immanuel Tabernacle(F.I.T). We are dedicated to meeting the needs of people holistically in the Mindoro Island Area and Beyond the region.

    WHY FAITH ?
    FAITH speaks the langguage of heart. It is an expression of HOPE the beyond the mind. "And it is impossible to please God without FAITH. Anyone who wants to come to him must believe that GOD exist and that he rewards those who sincerely seek him" (Hebrew 11:6). What you believe matters. Right believing is the door out of vicious cycle of defeat.

    FAITH provides help to children and families in need in holistic approach recieve practical and spritual support, Fueled by the support of the Church Community and ministries outside the country, Faith also providing equal opportunity to all. In this way we build confidence which reduces gap, poverty and social exclusion. We friendly environment where individual and families socially mix and support each other. We recognize the importance of holistic approach in improving the individual and family`s well being. We work with family as a whole, providing practical , emotional , psychological and spiritual support . We strengthen families by providing spiritual environment , promote family devotion and worship offer home bible study , create home church for families living in remote places.

Our Church Vission, Mission, and Goals

  • VISION

    STRENGTHENING A FAMILY, BUILDING A GODLY COMMUNITY.
    We are a non-profit organization with a big dream for helping those who are hopeless, to achieve a state of independence, sustainability and productivity.

  • MISSION

    FAITH is a temporary santuary, a place of refuge, protection, a place where people could find rest and experience FELLOWSHIP, WORSHIP, and SPIRITUAL FORMATION.
    DEDICATION, PASSION LOVE, Moving individuals and families in need in the communities of Victoria and beyond the regions from hopeless to wholesome

  • GOALS

    HOUSE OF PRAYER
    Transformation
    Bahay tuluyan (Temporary Shelter)
    for homeless children aged 1 to 12 and women
